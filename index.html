Hello! Welcome to the Cincinnati Crypto Meetup group website. Our goal is to transition everyone in Cincinnati and beyond from knowing absolutely nothing about cryptocurrency to being able to walk the walk and talk the talk with the big dogs. The way that we do this is by educating the masses, allowing them to comfortably embrace this new technology that is literally sweeping across the globe and empowering many lives. If you are in Cincinnati or any of the neighboring cities / states and would like to join in on our events whether in person or virtually, please Click Here to keep up to date on all of out upcoming meetup dates.

**This group is managed by the Black Males Mancave (BMM). A cincinnati based organization uplifting men and allowing them to feel safe and comforatble within our community. To learn more about the BMM please Click Here. For any questions or concerns regarding the meetup group, feel free to contact the event coordinator via email.
